# FAQ - xAI API Billing

Frequently asked questions about xAI API billing.

## Payment Methods

### What payment methods are accepted?

- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- Debit cards
- Corporate cards
- Wire transfer (Enterprise only)

### How do I add a payment method?

1. Go to Billing → Payment Methods
2. Click "Add Payment Method"
3. Enter card details
4. Save

### Can I use PayPal?

PayPal is not currently supported. Please use a credit or debit card.

## Prepaid Credits

### How do prepaid credits work?

- Purchase credits upfront
- Usage is deducted from your balance
- No overage charges until credits are exhausted
- Credits don't expire

### What happens when credits run out?

- You'll receive notifications as balance gets low
- When exhausted, API calls may fail unless monthly billing is enabled
- Add more credits or enable monthly billing to continue

### Can I get a refund for unused credits?

Contact support@x.ai to discuss refund options.

## Monthly Billing

### How does monthly billing work?

- Usage is tracked throughout the month
- Invoice generated at month end
- Payment due within payment terms
- Available when prepaid credits are exhausted

### When am I charged?

Invoices are generated at the end of each billing period (monthly).

### What if I don't pay my invoice?

- Late payment notices sent
- API access may be suspended
- Contact support if you need to discuss payment arrangements

## Pricing

### Where can I find current pricing?

Visit https://docs.x.ai/docs/models for current model pricing.

### How are tokens calculated?

- Input tokens: Text/images sent to the model
- Output tokens: Generated response text
- Reasoning tokens: Internal model reasoning (Grok 4)
- Cached tokens: Repeated content (discounted)

### Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees. You're charged for:
- Token usage
- Tool invocations (where applicable)
- Any additional services you enable

## Invoices

### Where can I download invoices?

Go to Billing → Invoices in the Console.

### What's included in an invoice?

- Billing period
- Usage breakdown by model
- Token counts and costs
- Tool invocation costs
- Total charges

### Can I get a custom invoice format?

Enterprise customers can request custom invoice formats.

## Cost Control

### How do I set spending limits?

1. Go to Billing → Budgets
2. Set monthly limit
3. Choose action when limit reached (alert, throttle, or block)

### Can I set alerts for spending?

Yes, configure alerts in Billing Settings for:
- Daily spend thresholds
- Weekly spend thresholds
- Monthly budget warnings

### How do I reduce costs?

- Use cheaper models for simple tasks
- Enable caching for repeated prompts
- Optimize prompt length
- Use streaming only when needed
- Monitor usage regularly

## Tax

### Can I add my VAT/tax ID?

Yes, go to Billing → Tax Information and enter your tax ID.

### Are prices shown with or without tax?

Prices shown are typically before tax. Applicable taxes are added based on your location.

## Disputes

### How do I dispute a charge?

Contact support@x.ai with:
- Invoice number
- Specific charges in question
- Reason for dispute

### What's the dispute resolution timeframe?

We aim to resolve billing disputes within 5-10 business days.

## Enterprise

### What special billing options do Enterprise plans have?

- Custom contracts
- Volume discounts
- Net payment terms (Net 30, Net 60)
- Dedicated billing contact
- Custom invoicing

### How do I get Enterprise pricing?

Contact sales@x.ai for Enterprise pricing discussions.
